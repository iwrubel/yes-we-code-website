About



"Yes, We Code!" is a student-organized club at Head-Royce School. It was founded by Isabel Wrubel in 2015 in collaboration with Brian Sea, Head-Royce's computer science teacher. The club is open to all applicants regardless of experience. If you'd like to join "Yes, We Code!", simply shoot us an email or find one of the club members in person.
